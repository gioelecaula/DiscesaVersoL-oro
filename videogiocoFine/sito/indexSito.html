Go down the slope, dodge and jump!

School prototype

A skier controlled by hand movements, 3 starting lives, hearts and super powers along the slope… and a quiz to earn one last chance.

This website simply explains how the game works: who created it, the main rules, the camera controls and how the extra-life quiz works.

Go to controls See all the rules

In short

Everything you need to understand the game in one minute.

  • Go down the slope avoiding obstacles.
  • Move right and left and jump over the jumpable ones.
  • You have 3 starting lives, but you can find hearts and super powers.
  • If you run out of lives, answer a quiz correctly to get one extra.
