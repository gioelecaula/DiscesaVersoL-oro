How to Play

Hand Controls

The game uses a camera to recognize your hand movements and control the skier in real time.

  • If you move your hand to the right, the skier moves right.
  • If you move your hand to the left, the skier moves left.
  • If you raise your hand, the character jumps over jumpable obstacles.
