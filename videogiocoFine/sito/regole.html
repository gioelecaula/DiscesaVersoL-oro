Game Rules

Gameplay

Everything the player needs to know before starting the descent.

  • The player controls a skier going down a snowy slope.
  • During the descent, different obstacles appear to avoid.
  • To avoid them you must:
    • Move to the right or to the left.
    • Jump over the obstacles that can be jumped.
  • The player starts with 3 lives.
  • Along the slope you can find:
    • Hearts that add one life.
    • Super powers that give temporary abilities (e.g. shield, turbo).
3 starting lives Heart: +1 life Shield: obstacle protection Turbo: increased speed Enhanced jump
